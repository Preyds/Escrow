Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal operational points required to process secure P2P transactions securely. This includes your email identifier, partner details, asset specifications, and transaction logs. Payment records are securely routed directly through Paystack API networks.

2. Financial Data Privacy

Tradescrow never holds, accesses, or views your bank account passwords, card pins, or full card details. All verification metadata and payouts are securely handled behind the scenes using Paystack's fully certified PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.

3. Data Retention

Transaction records and platform chat history logs are stored securely using Supabase database clustering. You can manage, review, or clear your session profile indicators by logging out of the dashboard platform.
