Terms of Service

Last updated: May 2026

1. Escrow Fee Structure

Tradescrow charges a standard flat 2% platform escrow fee on the total value of each initialized contract. This fee covers payment processing infrastructure, operational maintenance, and dispute mediation overhead costs. The fee responsibility is defined during the creation phase of the agreement.

2. Transaction Safety Rules

Users agree to use Tradescrow strictly for legal peer-to-peer (P2P) asset transfers. Buyers must verify the contents and security access details of the digital assets (such as gaming handles or profiles) completely before authorizing the final release of funds. Once funds are released by the buyer, the transaction is irreversibly finalized.

3. Dispute Settlement Policy

If an agreement enters a Disputed status, the escrowed funds are instantly frozen securely within the portal pipeline. Both parties must submit clear conversational transcripts, account access proofs, or video documentation to our official support channel. Tradescrow administrators retain final authority to rule on disputes and distribute funds to the rightful party based on empirical verification.
